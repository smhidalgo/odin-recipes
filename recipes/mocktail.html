Grapefruit-Ginger Kombucha Mocktail

Description

Anyone doing a dry challenge or looking to give up alcohol altogether? If you want a grown-up cocktail without the alcohol, you'll love this healthy pink Grapefruit-Ginger Kombucha Mocktail! I love my cocktails, but I also like to take a break occasionally. It can be tough to find nonalcoholic cocktails that have complex flavors and feel sophisticated, which is why I love this simple kombucha mocktail. The kombucha adds zing and gut-health benefits, and the best part is no hangover the next day! For another easy grapefruit mocktail, try my Zero- Proof Jalapeño Paloma.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup fresh squeezed pink grapefruit juice, from 1 pink grapefruit
  • ice
  • ½ cup ginger-lemon kombucha
  • 1 sprig rosemary

Steps

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice. Pour in the grapefruit juice and kombucha and stir gently to combine.
  2. Garnish with a sprig of rosemary.
